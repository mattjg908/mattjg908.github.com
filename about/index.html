About Me

Resume

I have been a remote full-stack developer for over 7 years. I specialize in Elixir and Ruby, Phoenix and Rails. I'm an open-source contributor and author of Harvard University's CorpusBuilder Ruby Client Gem. I enjoy doing test driven development, learning new tricks with PostgreSQL, and I love pair programming. I have worked for a number of high profile clients such as Netflix, Google and Harvard. I have been a Wall Street options trader (silver and gold), an English teacher, and a motorcycle mechanic. Lately I've been reading Testing Elixir and practing speed reading techniques.

Of course I also need to step away from the computer from time to time... outside of coding I do motorcycle stunts on my dirtbike and my msx. I also repair and restore vintage Japanese minitrucks like the Nissan D21 Hardbody and Gen 1 Toyota Tacomas.
